VALORANT

Free-to-play

Valorant is an online tactical shooter according to team 5 vs 5 and is a FPS game set in a near future. The player who controls agents (agents) comes from many countries and cultures in the world. With separate characteristics and skills. In the main mode (set of Spike), each team has five players who will choose agents with unique skills, so that it is suitable for the competition map and tactics for the team. Two teams are divided into two sides (Side): Attacker side or defense (defender side). Players use the currency in each game to recharge and buy armor for themselves, and buy and exchange weapons with teammates. A timer bomb is called Spike for the attack faction, what they need to do is within 100 seconds to put it in one of the spike areas of the map or destroy all the defense faction. If the attacking side destroys all the defensive faction or successfully defends Spike and let it explode, the attacking side of the game. If the defense successfully removed Spike within 45 seconds after Spike was placed or after 100 seconds that the attacking side had not placed Spike but the defensive faction was still there, the defensive side would win the game. In the first game, both sides have 45 seconds to prepare, with the next game, withdraw 30 seconds and continue until game 13 when the two teams change the side.
